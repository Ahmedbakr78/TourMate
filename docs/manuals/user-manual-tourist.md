# TourMate — Tourist User Manual

This guide explains how to use the **Tourist** app of TourMate (Angular SPA). Log in with a
tourist account at the login screen.

## 1. Login & First Run
1. Open the app → you are redirected to **Login**.
2. Enter your registered email and password → **Sign In**.
3. If your email is unverified, you will be prompted to **Verify Email** (enter the code sent to
   your inbox, or **Resend Verification**).
4. Forgot password? Use **Forgot Password** → receive code → **Reset Password**.
5. New here? **Register** a tourist account, then verify your email.

## 2. Home
- The **Home** screen is your landing dashboard after login.
- Quick links to plan trips, browse places, and view tips.

## 3. My Trips
- Open **My Trips** (trip list) to see trips you created.
- Tap a trip to open **Trip Detail**: itinerary, assigned guide/driver/vehicle, status, price,
  and actions (start, complete, cancel, share, duplicate).
- Use **Trip Calendar** to view trips on a calendar view.

## 4. New Trip Builder
1. Open **New Trip**.
2. Choose destinations/places, dates, number of travellers, and preferences.
3. The system **calculates the price** (Calculate Price).
4. **Assign a Guide**, **Assign a Driver**, and **Assign a Vehicle** (vehicle capacity must be ≥
   travellers).
5. Save → trip appears in **My Trips** with status *planned*.

## 5. Places
- **Places** lists discoverable Points of Interest (POIs).
- Use **Search**, **Filter**, **Nearby**, and **Popular** to find spots.
- Open **Place Detail** for description, location, reviews, and a **Save** (favorites) action.

## 6. Favorites
- **Favorites** shows places you saved. Remove by tapping the saved item.

## 7. Tools
- **Tools** provides utility features (e.g., converters/helpers) for travellers.

## 8. Tips
- **Tips** shows travel advice and recommendations.

## 9. Shared Trips
- **Shared Trips** displays trips shared with you by other tourists/admins.
- You can view (and, if permitted, join) co-traveller itineraries.

## 10. Lost & Found
1. Open **Lost & Found**.
2. **Report** a lost item on a trip (description, trip reference) → status *open*.
3. If found, mark **Report Found**; close or reopen as needed.
4. Track the lifecycle: open → found → closed.

## 11. Profile
- **Profile** shows your info. Edit details, upload a **profile image** (image files only),
  and **Delete Account** if desired.

## 12. Settings
- **Settings** lets you switch language (**English / العربية**) — Arabic enables **RTL** layout —
  and manage preferences. Change password via **Change Password**.

## 13. Notifications
- The **Notifications** center lists alerts (e.g., guide/driver approval, trip updates).
- Tap to **mark as read**, or **mark all read**; unread count shows in the header.

## Troubleshooting
- Can't log in: verify email first; use Forgot Password.
- Assignment rejected: check vehicle capacity and that guide/driver is approved & available.
- Upload failed: only image files are accepted; check file size limit.
